Tales of the Afterworld


is a story about a post-apocalyptic world now only occupied by infected monsters and mutated animals. One group of survivors found shelter in a mysterious tree, looked over by an even more mysterious person known only as "The Scientist"

The main part of this story will be told in an animated series! It is currently only being made by one person, so production will take a while.

For now, you can check out animated music videos that hint at what's to come, or you can get a closer look at the cast of the main series!

If you want to keep up with production of the animated series, I'll post all major updates here!
