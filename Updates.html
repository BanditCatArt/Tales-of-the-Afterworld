Update 10/28/2025

With another new music video for the series completed, I have learned a bit more about the process that I want to use for backgrounds for the pilot!

In the new music video, I 3D modeled Erin and Casey's rooms and used the models are a base for reference when drawing the final backgrounds. This allows me to draw the backgrounds much faster than before, as well as keep furniture placment and sizing very consistent!

With this new technique, making the pilot may take a bit longer, since I now have to 3D model all of the locations. However, future epsiodes may go way faster, since I'll already have a lot of the main locations made!

Update 7/6/2025

With two practice animations now done to get a feel for animating the characters, I am now ready to move on to the beginning of work on the pilot episode!

While I was making those animations, I was also writing the script for the series. At the time of writing this, I have two entire episodes written, and a pretty good idea of the next few. I've already started work on the animatic, and it's coming along well!

I expect the episode to be somewhere between 15-20 minutes based on the script. That means it will still be quite a while before it's even close to being released, but just beginning work on the series is already very exciting!
