ABOUT
"TALES OF THE AFTERWORLD"


Tales of the Afterworld is a project that tells the stories of a post-apocalyptic world that had a mysterious end. The main series is an animated series, but all the side stories and spin-offs will be in comics!

The animated series follows a group of survivors in the apocalypse. Each character has their own motivations and goals, but they must unite to fight a common enemy if they want any chance of surviving in the new world.

The comics range from spin-offs following characters from the main series on their own personal journey, to completely separate adventures with different characters that take place in the same universe!


ABOUT
THE CREATOR

The whole series is made entirely by me, Bandit Cat! I'm a self taught artist and animator, inspired mostly by the works of DeadlyComics and aimkid. I started out by animating short parts of songs by Lemon Demon, (which can be found here) and once I felt confident enough in my ability to animate, I decided to start working on this series!
